== Description ==

HootBoard is a beautiful bulletin board for your organization. Whether you are a workplace, university, government, or an organization there is now a better way to get members engaged, content organized & knowledge shared. Use a HootBoard to promote events, post classifieds, start discussions, share links, upload documents and more. Better yet, HootBoard seamlessly works across web, mobile, kiosks & your organization's digital signage. That way you have one method to communicate with your entire audience. And with this WordPress plugin you can add a HootBoard to your website in under a minute. So let's get started with our perpetual *FREE* plan for web & mobile access!
